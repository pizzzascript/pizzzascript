The Recipe

From raw dough to golden crust. Here's how the magic happens.

1

Prep the Dough

Discovery & Research

We knead through your goals, audience, and competitors. Every great website starts with understanding what makes your business unique.

⏱️ 2–3 days
A rushed dough never rises. Take time with this step — it shapes everything.
2

Choose Your Toppings

Design & Wireframing

You pick the flavors, I arrange them beautifully. Wireframes, mockups, and prototypes — tasted and approved before we fire up the oven.

⏱️ 3–5 days
Less can be more. The best pizzas don't need 47 toppings.
3

Into the Oven

Development

Code is written. Pixels are placed. The kitchen gets hot. This is where the recipe transforms from ingredients into something real.

⏱️ 1–4 weeks
I keep the oven door closed — no peeking! But I'll send you progress updates.

Quality Check

Testing & Review

No burnt edges. No raw centers. Every page is tested across devices and browsers. Your feedback is the final seasoning before we serve.

⏱️ 2–3 days
I test on real devices, not just the ones I like. Your users won't all have the latest iPhone.
5

Serve Hot

Launch & Delivery

Fresh out of the oven, straight to your domain. Deployed, optimized, and ready for your audience to devour.

⏱️ 1 day
Launching is the beginning, not the end. I stick around for seconds.
